MEDIUM LEVEL (7 questions)
================================================================================

1.  Explain how the traditional food items consumed in a particular state are closely related to the types of crops grown and the climatic conditions prevalent in that region.

2.  Describe two significant changes in culinary practices that have occurred over time, and explain one primary reason that has driven these changes.

3.  Why are carbohydrates and fats primarily referred to as "energy-giving foods," and how do their respective roles in providing energy to the body differ?

4.  How does choosing to eat food that is locally grown help in reducing "food miles," and what positive impact does this have on both the environment and local producers?

5.  Describe the observable change that indicates the presence of starch in a food item during a test, and briefly explain the significance of conducting such tests on food.

6.  What characteristics make millets known as "nutri-cereals," and how do they offer benefits not only for human health but also for the environment?

7.  How does wasting food impact the significant time and effort invested by farmers and other community members in bringing food from the farm to our plates, and what simple action can individuals take to prevent this wastage?
